# Data Analyst Market Expectations

Data analyst resumes should show SQL, spreadsheet analysis, dashboarding, business metrics, stakeholder communication, and decisions influenced by analysis.

Common signals include SQL joins, window functions, Excel, Google Sheets, Python, Pandas, Power BI, Tableau, Looker, data cleaning, ETL, KPI reporting, A/B testing, cohort analysis, funnel analysis, and storytelling.

Hiring teams value business context. A resume should explain the metric, audience, analysis method, and business action rather than only listing tools.

Good data analyst bullets:

- Built Power BI dashboards tracking revenue, conversion, and retention KPIs for weekly leadership reviews.
- Used SQL joins and window functions to analyze customer behavior, identify drop-off points, and recommend funnel improvements.
- Cleaned and transformed raw operational data with Python and Pandas, reducing manual reporting time.
- Presented insights to non-technical stakeholders and converted analysis into prioritised business actions.

Entry-level candidates should include portfolio projects with real datasets, clear questions, SQL notebooks, dashboard screenshots, and a short explanation of business findings.
